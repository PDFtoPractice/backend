<textline bbox="255.803,718.235,428.755,737.030">What is an angioplasty operation?</textline>
<textline bbox="255.803,705.622,410.306,721.606">An angioplasty operation aims to open </textline>
<textline bbox="255.803,695.623,442.317,711.607">blocked arteries around the heart. A doctor will </textline>
